Chicken Noodles

Idk what description I can write for chicken noodles.
I guess it is just something you need to have to truly appreciate.
Or maybe I'm too awful at describing food. Lol!

    Ingredients
  • Thin chicken strips
  • Pepper powder
  • Salt
  • Soya sauce
  • Spice powder
  • Water
Step - 1
Cut chicken to thin strips or bite sized pieces.
Large chunks of chicken will not get cooked faster and tends to turn hard.
Add the chicken to a bowl. Add in pepper powder, salt, soya sauce and spice powder
– five spice powder or garam masala.
Step - 2
Marinate this well. Cover and set aside till the noodles are cooked.
You can also refrigerate this upto 24 hours and use when needed.
In restaurants they also use little corn
starch or potato starch or even egg to coat the chicken.
This helps to retain the juices and yield very tender and soft chicken.
